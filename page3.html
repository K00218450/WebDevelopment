Headphones

Quite Comfort 35

The Quite Comfort 35 are wireless headphones that are developed by BOSE.THE QC 35 are noise cancelling headphones. This is done by speakers on the outside that drown out Noises like trains and cars the headphones are Bluetooth so they connect wirelessly but also has an Aux port for cable option. The battery can last for 20 hours and a 15-minute charge can last 2.5 hours The QC 35 is extremely durable and flexible which makes them very comfortable. The headphones sell for €379.

Solo 3

The solo 3 headphones are wireless headphones that were developed by beats which is a company owned by apple. The solo 3 have appless w1 chip that makes pairing with apple devices a lot easier and faster than using regular Bluetooth paring. The solo3 has a slick design when being worn. Many adjustments can be made for owner’s comfort. The solo3 has a very fast charge time with only 5-minute charge can last up to 3 hours and 40 hours on a full charge. There are also 8 colors to pick from e.g. apples product red and rose gold. The solo3 sells at around €299.
