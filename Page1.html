Phones

Iphone

The IPhone 6 was the sixth installment in Apple inc's smartphone devices. It was announced September 9 2014 and released 10 days later. Apples first smartphone was the IPhone released in June 2007 and sold over 6.1 million units. When the IPhone 6 was released it sold 10 million units its first weekend. Today it has over 250 million units sold. The Phone came with apples 10s8 it has Optical image stabilization with 1080p 60fps video recording camera. The smartphone has a touch ID sensor to make security fast and easy. The phone came in white, rose gold, gold and space grey. Each unit sold for €669.

OnePLus3

The OnePlus 3 is one of the newest smartphones by One Plus and is a successor to the One plus 2. Revealed in June 14 2016 and was on sale the same day the one plus 3 is the first phone that comes with a dash charge feature "A days Power in Half An Hour" The phone was selling at a very cheap price compared to other smart phones with very similar specs the one plus 3 was selling at €399 but was discontinued early 2017 with the release of the one plus 3T.
