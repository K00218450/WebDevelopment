Consoles

Play Station 4

The PS4 is a very popular console. The ps4 had sold more than 53 million consoles from its launch in November 2013. Other top selling consoles would be the Xbox one and Wii U. The Ps4 was developed by song computer entertainment. The ps4 is a predecessor to the ps3 which sold over 83 million units. Play Station has its own online feature called PSN (Play Station Network) it also has an online store (pss) it is an online shop where PlayStation users can buy digital copies of new games and are usually cheaper than Disk forms copies of the original copy of a game. Plat Station Plus is a subscription service on PSN it sells for €50 a year with this you can play online multiplayer get 24 ps4 games a year for no extra cost and access to exclusive discounts.

Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo switch is Nintendo’s latest console to be released it was reveled in October 2016 and was released on march 2017 and has sold over 1.5 million units in its first week. The Nintendo switch has many ways of being played it can be connected to a TV by a docking station that holds the screen and be removed to be used as a tablet by using its touch screen. The Switch’s main way of being played is with its Joycon controllers with motion sensing tactile feedback these can be attached to the console and used as a portable device.
